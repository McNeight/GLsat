Moving Light Source

Description

The example shows a simple torus drawn in red. At the center of the torus is a white cube depicting the position of the light source. The left mouse button starts the animation and you see the light move as the animation progresses. Notice the specular hightlight shift from the top of the torus to the bottom, become invisible (when the light moves to the back of the torus) and then become visible again. Further, note how the ambient lighting keeps changing between the front and the back of the scene.
