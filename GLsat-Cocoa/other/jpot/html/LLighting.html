With and Without Lighting

Idea:

Lighting and its appropriate use can create very real effects when rendering 3D objects. In fact, without lighting objects tend not to look realistic at all. With OpenGL, we can manipulate lighting in multiple ways.

Enabling / Disabling Lighting

Lighting is enabled by calls to the glEnable(GL_LIGHTING) and glEnable(GL_LIGHTi) routines. Here GL_LIGHTi represents the i-th light source (i starts from 0).. A single light source is disabled with glDisable(GL_LIGHTi). To disable all lighting use glDisable(GL_LIGHTING).
