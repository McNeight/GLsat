Orthographic Projection

Idea

Instead of perspective projection one could use orthographic projection whose viewing volume is a rectangular box shown below. In orthographic projection the objects in the box are projected toward the near clipping plane without forshortening of perspective projection. To specify the viewing box we imagine that is is centered around the z axix and use

glOrtho(left, right, bottom, top, near, far);

where (left,bottom,-near) and (right,top,-near) are the lower-left and upper-right corners of the near clipping plane, and (left,bottom,-far) and (right,top,-far) are the same respective corners of the far clipping plane. Both near and far can be positive or negative.
